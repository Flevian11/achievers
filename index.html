Features

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to join Achievers ?

Membership is open to all persons above the age of 18 with an income.

How much loan can I qualify for ?

The maximum loan amount a member can be given at any one time is three times the member’s savings.

When do I qualify for a loan ?

A member is eligible for a loan after 3 months of consecutive contribution and saving..

What do I need when applying for a loan ?

A member when signing in provides his/her credentials which the team uses for loan verification....

Can I boost deposits and take loans directly ?

Yes. However, a one off commission of 2.5% on the topped up deposits is charged if the member takes a loan within 6 months of boosting those deposits.

How long will my loan take before disbursement ?

The system uses an AI automated engine for loan allocation. Disbursement period ranges from 24 - 72 hrs.
